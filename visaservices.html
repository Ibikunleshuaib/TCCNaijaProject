TYPES OF VISA IN NIGERIA

There are various types of visa in Nigeria. Each type is differentiated from the other on the basis of what will be the duration of the visa. The different types of visa are given below:

1.Transit visa

Also known as a travel visa, a transit visa is a document which allows a person to stay in a country while completing his journey to any other country. Usually, the duration of the stoppage is mentioned in the visa and is normally under 24 hours. This type of Visa is required by a traveller who needs to change his flight or any other means of transportation without taking the hassle of immigration control.

Documents required for the transit Visa
  • • A passport which is valid for a minimum of 6 months consists of at least 2 blank visa pages.
  • • Two recent passport sized photographs.
  • • A confirmed ticket of a connecting flight which is already booked
  • • Evidence of sufficient funds
Procedure to avail Transit Visa in Nigeria

There are two ways to apply for a transit Visa:

  • 1. Receive a transit Nigeria visa on arrival at the airport in Nigeria by submitting a request of a transit Visa and then submitting the required fees electronically.
  • 2. By following the steps given below
    • 1. Apply online, fill the application and download the completed application.
    • 2. Pay online and get a receipt of the payment
    • 3. Fix 2 passport size photo to the visa form IMM22 along with the other required documents and submit either via post or in person.

    • It is usually for a period of 48 hours and can be extended up to 7 days under extreme situations.

2.Tourist Visa

This is a visa which will authorize a person to visit Nigeria for the purpose of tourism. This visa can also be used if a foreigner wants to visit friends or family in the country for a few days. It is to be needed that citizen of all the countries except the members of ECOWAS countries and the citizen of those countries with which Nigeria has entered into Visa abolition agreements.

Documents required for Tourist Visa in Nigeria
  • • A passport which is valid for a minimum of 6 months consists of at least 2 blank visa pages.
  • • A return ticket
  • • Evidence that the person will not run out of funds during the journey
  • • Proof of the hotel reservation or the address of the host in Nigeria

3. Business Visa

People who want to travel to Nigeria to attend or conduct Meeting, Conference, Seminar, Contract Negotiation, Marketing, Sales, Purchase distribution of Nigerian Goods, Trade Fairs, Job Interviews, Humanitarian Services, Emergency or Relief work, Musical Concert, etc. need to avail a Business Visa in Nigeria.

Documents required for a Business Visa in Nigeria

  • • Two recent passport sized photographs.
  • • A return ticket

  • • Proof of the hotel reservation or the address of the host in Nigeria
Procedure to Avail a Business Visa in Nigeria

Follow the steps given below to apply for a Business visa in Nigeria:

  • 1. Apply for the visa form online, complete and print the form
  • 2. Pay online and get a receipt of the payment
  • 3. Fix 2 passport size photo to the visa form IMM22 along with the other required documents and submit either via post or in person.

  • 4. Temporary Work Permit (TWP)

    When experts in a field of study are invited by any business entity so that they could training the employees of the company, they require a Temporary Work Permit or TWP. This kind of visa is valid for a period of 90 days.

Documents required for a TWP in Nigeria
  • • Visa approval Letter (pre-approved visa letter) valid for 90 days from the date of issuance
  • • A passport which is valid for a minimum of 6 months consists of at least 2 blank visa pages.
  • • Two recent passport sized photographs.
  • • Completed Visa Form IMM22
  • • Proof of payment of Visa fee
Procedure to Avail TWP in Nigeria
  • • The business entity located in Nigeria files an official request on your behalf stating all the necessary details (name, nationality, passport number, purpose and duration, etc.)
  • • Submit the application after attaching all the required documents such as certificate of incorporation, CAC 2 & 7, and any other document required.
  • • Pay online and get a receipt of the payment
  • • Fix 2 passport size photo to the visa form IMM22 along with the other required documents and submit either via post or in person.

This type of Visa is issued according to the dates specified by the person in his or her contract. Normally it is only valid for a single entry to the country. This visa is usually utilized by Research Fellows, Foreign Students, Missionaries, Government Officials, etc.

Documents Required to avail an STR visa in Nigeria

  • • Two recent passport sized photographs.

6.Diplomatic Visa

This visa is applicable to the top government officials such as Visiting Heads of States and their families, Top officials of Government and their families, Accredited Diplomats and their families, Holders of United Nations / International Agencies Diplomatic Passport, etc.
